# Privacy Policy for Lifestacks.ai (including Relationship Manager)

**Last updated: April 26, 2026**

This Privacy Policy describes how Lifestacks.ai ("we," "us," "our") collects, uses, processes, and discloses information when you use our Life Stacks applications (the "Apps" or "Service"), including the Relationship Manager module and other productivity modules.

By using the Apps, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree, you should not use the Apps.

---

## 1. Introduction & Scope

**Purpose.** The Life Stacks platform helps users organize personal productivity through various modules including:

- **Relationship Manager**: Contact and relationship notes, goals alignment, optional event suggestions, AI-assisted drafting, and **media and files you upload yourself** (for example photos, documents, and message screenshots) stored for your account. Relationship Manager does **not** connect to external cloud photo libraries; uploads are only what you provide in the App.
- **Habit Master**: Tracks and optimizes personal habits using psychological frameworks
- **Focus Enhancer**: Analyzes screen time and provides therapeutic conversations for digital wellness
- **Analytics Dashboard**: Provides insights into productivity and goal achievement
- **Business Hacks**: Various productivity and business management tools

**Third-party sign-in.** Where we offer sign-in with a third-party identity provider, authentication is handled by that provider under their terms. That sign-in is separate from any files you upload inside Relationship Manager.

**Updates & consent.** If our data practices change in a material way, we will update this policy and, where required, obtain appropriate consent before new uses of your data.

## 2. What Information We Collect

### A. Information You Provide Directly

- **Account & contact info.** Your name, email address, profile details from your chosen sign-in method, and any username you choose.
- **App-specific data.** Notes, labels, metadata, tags, relationships, habits, goals, projects, tasks, and other data you enter into our applications.
- **User-uploaded content in Relationship Manager.** Photos, documents (for example plans or exports you upload), and screenshots you choose to upload. We store the files and associated metadata (such as filenames, timestamps, and AI-generated summaries you trigger in the product) to power features you request.

### B. Technical / Usage Data

- Device, browser, operating system, and version.
- IP address, approximate geolocation, and network identifiers.
- Usage logs: date/time of access, features used, errors, performance metrics.
- Cookies and similar technologies (for authentication, analytics, sessions).

## 3. How We Use the Information

We use your data for:

- **Providing the service.** Displaying, organizing, and managing your relationship data, habits, goals, projects, uploaded files, and metadata as intended.
- **AI-powered features.** Using your data to provide personalized insights, recommendations, drafts, and analysis through AI providers you enable (for example OpenAI), only as needed to fulfill the feature you use.
- **User experience improvements.** Aggregated analytics and performance metrics to improve features, UI, and reliability.
- **Support and communication.** To respond to inquiries, send updates, notifications, or security alerts.
- **Security, fraud prevention, compliance.** To detect abuse or unauthorized access, enforce our Terms of Service, and comply with legal obligations.

We do not:

- Sell your personal information to third parties for their own marketing.
- Use Relationship Manager uploads to train third-party foundation models beyond what your chosen AI provider’s terms allow for API usage, except as we disclose in product settings or agreements.
